# Shover.car

Shover is a special car. 

it is invulnerable to damage

its tires don't wear out

it doesn't run out of fuel

it likes to live in the middle of the race track

it have 360 degree vision with unlimited range

if it sees a wrecked car on the racing surface it will drive over to it and push it off the track then return to the middle of the track

if it sees a stalled car on the racing surface it will drive over to it and push it until the car frees itself then returns to the middle of the track. This does not damage the stalled car, it just pushes it until it can move again.

other cars will try to avoid it while its on the racing surface. They will take damage if they hit it.
